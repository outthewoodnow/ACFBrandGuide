Overview

This guide defines the core visual and verbal system for Athlete Connections Foundation. It is designed to be practical for quick execution across web, print, and social touchpoints.

Brand Summary

Athlete Connections champions youth development through sport, life skills, and education pathways. Use this guide to maintain consistency and clarity across every public-facing asset.

Tagline: Empowering Youth Through Sports, Life Skills & Education.

Layout & Grid

  • Use a grid-based structure.
  • Maintain generous whitespace.
  • Keep content left aligned for readability.
  • A vertical line/divider motif may be used to separate hierarchy.

Empowering Youth Through Sports

Practical mentorship and education programs create pathways from potential to progress.

Voice

Tone: Clear, encouraging, and credible. Speak with confidence while staying human and action-oriented.

Message style: Lead with impact, ground claims in real outcomes, and keep language direct and accessible to families, partners, and youth.

Do Nots

  • No gradients.
  • No shadows.
  • No rotation or distortion of logos.
  • No unapproved colors.
  • No busy decorative marks.
